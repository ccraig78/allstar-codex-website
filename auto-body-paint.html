Body repair and refinishing belong in the same plan.

A good-looking repair starts before paint is applied. Dents, creases, scrapes, panel condition, and fitment all influence the preparation and refinishing work. All-Star reviews the visible damage and the finish you need before building the estimate.

Services can include body repair, paint matching, refinishing, panel paint, touch-ups, and paint work connected to collision or bumper repairs. Full repaint needs are quoted according to vehicle condition and the desired finish.

Paint and body questions

What to know before requesting a quote.

Can paint damage be quoted from a photo?

Photos are a useful start, but lighting and hidden panel damage can affect the final scope. An onsite look may be needed for an accurate quote.

Do you repair the body work before painting?

The repair plan accounts for panel condition and preparation before refinishing. The exact steps depend on the damage found.

Do you offer custom paint?

Yes, custom paint and specialty refinishing projects are considered by estimate. Send photos and describe the finish you have in mind.
